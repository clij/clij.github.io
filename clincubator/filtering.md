# Filtering
CLIncubator comes with filters for 
* noise removal
  * Mean
  * Median
  * Gaussian blur
* background subtraction
  * Top Hat (similar to ImageJs "Subtract Background")
  * Subtract Gaussian background
* mixed
  * Difference of Gaussian (DoG)
  * Laplacien of Gaussian (LoG)
